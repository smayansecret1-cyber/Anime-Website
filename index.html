🎥🌸 What is Anime? 🎥🌸

Anime is a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular all over the world. It includes genres like action, adventure, romance, comedy, fantasy, horror, and science fiction. Unlike regular cartoons made mainly for children, anime is created for all age groups. It is known for its unique art style, expressive characters, emotional storytelling, and intense fight scenes. Many series are adapted from Japanese comics called manga. Over time, anime has become a global phenomenon inspiring movies, games, fan art, and conventions.
